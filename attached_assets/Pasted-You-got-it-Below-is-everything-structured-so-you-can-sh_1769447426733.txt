3️⃣ FIRST COACH OUTREACH SCRIPT (THIS MATTERS)

This is not salesy. It’s peer-to-peer.

DM / Email Script

Hey Coach —
I built a small swing analysis tool focused on decision timing, not mechanics.
It explains why swings break down in games instead of just showing angles.
I’m not trying to replace coaching — just pressure-test the feedback with real coaches.
Would you be open to taking a look and telling me if the language makes sense?

That’s it.
No links unless they say yes.

If they ask “What’s different?”

Say this verbatim:

Most tools show what a swing looks like.
This explains when a hitter commits and what that costs them against real pitches.

Stop talking after that.
